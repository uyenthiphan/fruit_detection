Machine Learning fruit quality (Rotten and healthy) detection.

For strawberries, peaches and pomegranates.

According to WFP, all the food produced but never consumed would be enough to feed two billion people, which represents more than double the number of malnourished people worldwide.

Automating production, storage and distribution processes can help reduce waste and hunger.

This project provides 3 verification options: individual image, group of images and camera capture.
